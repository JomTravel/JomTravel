ABOUT US

OUR TEAM

Our organisation was founded on 18 July 2022 and consists of Tan Chin Yong, Tan Chi Haur, Luah Zheng Sheng, Luah Zheng Yang and the team leader Tang Lit Xuan. The team leader is mainly responsible for assisting and giving advice or guidance to the team members and assigning tasks. Tan Chin Yong, Luah Zheng Sheng, Luah Zheng Yang and Tan Chi Haur were responsible for building the structure of the website and designing the look and feel of the website, while Tan Chi Haur was responsible for gathering various informations to assist the team in the development of the website.

OUR PURPOSE

Our original intention in developing the website was to make Malaysia known to more people, especially foreigners. It also allows users to get to know all the state destinations in Malaysia at once. To make travelling in Malaysia easier and more convenient for users. Our website is a collection of tourist destinations in all the states of Malaysia, with an intuitive and fresh interface, so that users can easily understand and select the places they want to visit.
