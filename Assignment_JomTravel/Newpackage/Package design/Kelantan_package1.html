Beach of Passionate Love

Beach of Passionate Love was believe to name by a lovelorn British soldier during the World War Two. Till today, there are some locals still prefer to us the old name.

It has a beautiful long and wide sandy beach, a popular spot for family outings and picnics. It also have a variety of water sports and horse riding.

There are some food stalls and restaurants along the beach, which offers fresh barbeque seafood.

Gua Ikan

Gua Ikan is the name of a cave complex at Bukit Batu Kapur about 5 km south of Dabong.

There a number of caves here but most are not easy to access and it is recommended to engage a guide in order to appreciate the full extent of this complex.

If there is no guide available at the cave complex it might be possible to hire someone from the nearby Gunung Stong State Park.
