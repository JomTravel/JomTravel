Sungai Petani

Pathini was the name of a daughter of Merong Mahawangsa, founder of the preceding Langkasuka Empire.

The word Petani might be related to the province of Pattani in Thailand. The word is said to derive from a Sanskrit word 'pathini', meaning "virgin nymph";.

The name of the municipality is taken from the name of a river, which is Sungai Petani (Petani River), a tributary of Merbok River.
