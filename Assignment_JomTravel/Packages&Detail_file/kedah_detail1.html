Schedule

Day Time Event
1 20:00pm Arrival at Langkawi (Langkawi) International Airport, your guide will transfer you to the hotel of your choice.
2 07:00am Breakfast at the hotel
09:00am Go to explore Beras Basah Island, a unique island with crystal clear waters and sandy beaches, arrive at the island mangroves, the boatman starts throwing food to the sea so that everyone can watch and take pictures of the eagles preying on them, you can enjoy fishing here
11:00am Then head to Pulau Dayung Bunting, the island of pregnant women. Upon arrival, walk about 10 minutes on the mountain road to Maternity Lake. (photographs and rental of play equipment are available)
13.00pm Enjoy a seafood BBQ meal at White Beach with all-you-can-drink
17.00pm Back to the pier, the driver will take everyone back to the hotel
3 07.30am Breakfast at the hotel
09.00am Go to Oriental Village, when you arrive at Oriental Village, you can visit the 3D Art Museum (3D Art Museum Langkawi), take the cable car to the highest mountain and step on the most famous Sky Bridge in Langkawi
13.00pm Lunch at Oriental Village (Optional)
16.00pm Go to Perdana Gallery, Wildlife Park, Eagle Square, Pantai Cenang
18.00pm Go to Langkawi Kuah Town Duty Free Shop. End today's tour and return to your hotel.
4 08.00am Breakfast at the hotel
10.00am Free time or enjoy shopping in the city and bring back Malaysian gifts for your family or friends.
15.00pm Transfer to Langkawi International Airport for flight back to warm home
